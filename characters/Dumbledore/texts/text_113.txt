Harry:「And Kreacher told you all this . . . and laughed?」
Dumbledore:「He did not wish to tell me. But I am a sufficiently accomplished Legilimens myself to know when I am being lied to and I — persuaded him — to tell me the full story, before I left for the Department of Mysteries.」
Harry:「And, and Hermione kept telling us to be nice to him —」
Dumbledore:「She was quite right, Harry, I warned Sirius when we adopted twelve Grimmauld Place as our headquarters that Kreacher must be treated with kindness and respect. I also told him that Kreacher could be dangerous to us. I do not think that Sirius took me very seriously, or that he ever saw Kreacher as a being with feelings as acute as a human’s —」
