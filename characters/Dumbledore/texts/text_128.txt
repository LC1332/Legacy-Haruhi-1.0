Harry:「But while I was at the Dursleys’ . . . interrupted Harry, his voice growing stronger, I realized I can’t shut myself away or — or crack up. Sirius wouldn’t have wanted that, would he? And anyway, life’s too short. . . . Look at Madam Bones, look at Emmeline Vance. . . . It could be me next, couldn’t it?
