Nearly Headless Nick:「Oh the usual, Wreaked havoc and mayhem. Pots and pans everywhere. Place swimming in soup. Terrified the house-elves out of their wits —」

Nearly Headless Nick:「Certainly, The largest number in any dwelling in Britain, I believe. Over a hundred.」
Hermione:「I’ve never seen one!」
